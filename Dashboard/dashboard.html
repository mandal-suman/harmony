Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new habit?
To create a new habit, navigate to the Habits section and click the "Create New Habit" button. Fill in the habit name, frequency, and preferred time of day. Your habit will then appear in your habit tracker with a progress bar.
Can I edit or delete a goal?
Yes, you can edit or delete any goal. In the Goals section, click on the three-dot menu next to any goal to access editing options. You can modify the title, description, due date, or delete the goal entirely.
How is my productivity score calculated?
Your productivity score is based on several factors including task completion rate, habit consistency, goal progress, and journaling frequency. The algorithm weighs recent activity more heavily to provide an up-to-date assessment of your productivity.
Is my data private and secure?
Absolutely. We take your privacy seriously. All your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We never share your personal information with third parties, and you have full control over your data at all times.
